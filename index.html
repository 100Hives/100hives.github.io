Desk Worker Relief

Office Wellness

Break up your desk work with these mobility exercises to prevent back pain and improve posture.

Thoracic Openers

  • Interlock hands behind head
  • Round mid-back, bring elbows together
  • Hold for 5 seconds, repeat 10 times
  • Perfect after every hour of sitting

Dead Bugs

  • 90-degree hip and knee position
  • Lower opposite leg and arm slowly
  • Hold 5 seconds, 10 reps each side
  • Strengthens core stability

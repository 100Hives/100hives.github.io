Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and booking

How quickly can I get an appointment?

We typically have availability within 48-72 hours. For urgent cases or acute injuries, we offer same-day appointments when possible. We recommend booking online or calling us directly to check real-time availability.

Do you offer mobile services?

Yes! We provide mobile therapy services across Feltham and surrounding areas. This includes deep tissue massage, sports massage, dry cupping, and injury rehabilitation in the comfort of your home.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring comfortable clothing you can move in, any relevant medical reports or doctor's notes, and a list of current medications. We'll provide towels and all necessary equipment.

How do I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment by calling us or using your online account. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

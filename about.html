Our Story

Release Therapies was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional therapeutic services that help people move better, feel better, and live better.

What began as a small practice with just two therapists has grown into a comprehensive wellness center with a team of dedicated professionals, all committed to delivering personalized care and effective treatment plans.

Our approach combines evidence-based techniques with a holistic understanding of the body and mind. We believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms, and in empowering our clients with the knowledge and tools they need to maintain their health and wellness long-term.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, appointments, and policies.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit will include a thorough assessment of your concerns, health history, and goals. We'll discuss your treatment options and develop an initial plan. Depending on the service, we may begin treatment during this first session. Please arrive 15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

How long are the treatment sessions?

Session lengths vary depending on the service and your specific needs. Massage therapy sessions typically range from 30 to 90 minutes. Physical therapy sessions are usually 45 to 60 minutes. Specialized treatments may have different durations, which will be discussed when scheduling your appointment.

Do you accept insurance?

We accept many major insurance plans for physical therapy services. For massage therapy, we can provide a detailed receipt that you may submit to your insurance for possible reimbursement. We recommend contacting your insurance provider before your appointment to verify coverage. Our staff is also happy to help you understand your benefits.

What should I wear to my appointment?

For massage therapy, you'll undress to your comfort level and will be properly draped throughout the session. For physical therapy, wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothes that allow for movement and access to the area being treated. Athletic wear or gym clothes are ideal.

How often should I schedule appointments?

The frequency of appointments depends on your specific condition, goals, and treatment plan. Some clients benefit from weekly sessions, while others may need bi-weekly or monthly visits. Your therapist will recommend an appropriate schedule based on your individual needs and adjust as you progress.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that schedules change, but we kindly request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service price. No-shows will be charged the full service price.

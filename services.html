Comprehensive Therapy Services

At Release Therapies, we offer a wide range of therapeutic services designed to address your specific needs. Our experienced therapists are dedicated to helping you achieve optimal health and wellness through personalized care.

Professional Treatment Environment

We provide a calm, comfortable space with professional-grade equipment to ensure you receive the highest quality care during your therapy sessions.

Massage Therapy

Deep Tissue Massage

A therapeutic massage that focuses on realigning deeper layers of muscles and connective tissue. Especially helpful for chronic aches and pains, and contracted areas such as stiff neck, low back tightness, and sore shoulders.

Swedish Massage

A gentle form of massage that uses long strokes, kneading, deep circular movements, vibration and tapping. Perfect for those who are new to massage, want to relieve muscle tension, or simply need to relax and rejuvenate.

Duration: 30, 60, or 90 minutes
Price: From £60

Sports Massage

Designed for athletes and active individuals, sports massage focuses on areas of the body that are overused and stressed from repetitive movements. Helps prevent injuries, prepare the body for athletic activity, and maintain optimal condition.

Duration: 30, 60, or 90 minutes
Price: From £70

NHS & Private Healthcare

We provide both NHS referral services and private treatments to ensure you get the care you need when you need it.

NHS Services

We work with NHS referrals for physiotherapy and manual therapy services. Bring your GP referral letter to access NHS-funded treatments.

What you need:

  • Valid GP referral letter
  • NHS number
  • Photo ID (passport or driving licence)

Note: NHS massage therapy is typically limited to medical necessity. For wellness and relaxation massage, private payment applies.

Private Healthcare Insurance

We accept most UK private healthcare insurance providers. Please check with your provider for coverage details and obtain pre-authorization if required.

  • BUPA
  • AXA Health (formerly PPP Healthcare)
  • Aviva Health
  • Vitality Health
  • WPA
  • Simply Health
  • And many others...

For private insurance claims, we can provide detailed receipts and clinical notes to support your claim submission.

Private Payment Options

For private treatments and self-funded sessions, we accept:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Cash
  • Bank Transfer
  • Apple Pay & Google Pay
  • Healthcare Cash Plans (receipts provided)

Payment Policies:

  • Private appointments: Payment required at time of service
  • Package deals: Payment required upfront with significant savings
  • Cancellations: 24-hour notice required to avoid fees
  • Late arrivals: May result in shortened sessions
